FAQ

What information should I add to my family profile?

When customizing your family profile, share information that you and your family are comfortable with sharing. As a family-friendly website, we prioritize the feeling of safety for every family.

How do we chat with a connection?

Our purpose is to quicken the process of finding new connections. We do not have a chat system and instead provide a Contact Info section where you can express your preferred methods of communication.

Is filling out the Contact Info section required?

Setting up a family profile requires only a family nickname. Any other section on the family profile is optional for you to fill out. Note that the Contact Info section is only viewable to your connections.

When is it oversharing?

Before sharing any personal information, understand that your information will be viewable to other families on the website. Only share what you would share when meeting someone the first time.

How do I limit what others can see on my family profile?

You can hide your family profile by selecting the 'Hide Family Profile' option in the Visibility menu. This menu is available on the right side of your family profile header. Note that when you are hidden, others cannot send you a connect request, nor can you send a request to another family.

Are there any age requirement for joining Family+?

You must be over 18 years old to join.

How much does it costs to join?

It is free to join!
